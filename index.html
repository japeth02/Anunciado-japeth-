Welcome to Amanleya Garden Resort

Amanlayan Beach Resort, located in Cateel, Davao Oriental, offers a picturesque
coastal experience with pristine white sands and azure waters. The resort features palm trees and panoramic ocean views, inviting guests to create
their own narrative of relaxation and adventure amidst the beauty of
the Philippines' natural splendor.

Our exclusive resort caters to adults seeking a romantic escape, offering intimate accommodations and exquisite dining experiences overlooking the tranquil sea. Indulge in sunrise strolls along the shore, rejuvenating spa treatments, and personalized wellness offerings designed to enhance your sense of serenity and well-being. Come discover the epitome of luxury and seclusion at Amanlayan Beach Resort, where every moment is a cherished memory in the making.
